Funded by a company noone to this day still has ever heard of

This was the corporation that was created to oversee the funding sources, not the source of the funding. Every major development project has its own corporate entity.

AuAg plate right below the monument with the Masonic logo

This is the cornerstone dedication. A sizeable number of public structures have a cornerstone dedication performed by the local Masons. It's done for hospitals, schools, and any sort of structure erected for the public good and has been for over 200 years. The cornerstone of the Massachusetts statehouse was just rededicated by the Grand Lodge of Masons in Massachusetts with 1,300 Masons in attendance, echoing the original dedication that was presided over by Grand Master of Masons in Massachusetts, Paul Revere and Governor Sam Adams.

There's nothing unique about this airport in that respect.

52sq miles covered yet less runways than the previous airport
