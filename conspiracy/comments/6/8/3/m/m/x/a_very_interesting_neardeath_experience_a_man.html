A very interesting near-death experience. A man meets the Demiurge before coming back to his body on Earth

30  2017-04-28 by ArchonFall4All

I found an amazing OBE story yesterday that I thought most people here would be interested in. Here's a link to what is the transcript of an almost 3 hour interview. Of course, it's quite a long read so I'll try to highlight some of the main ideas.

The majority of the interview sounds like a typical near death experience. The man has a car accident, he dies, and then his life flashes before his eyes where he sees highlights of his life and the love he experienced/shared in this world. He's then in a realm of complete darkness before seeing the light that leads him back to "heaven." It's here that he experiences the true light and love of the real God/creator/source. He finds out that he has more to learn and experience about love so he's ultimately sent back to Earth.

Here's where things get interesting. His soul is flying through the cosmos on its way back to his body, and he quickly approaches Earth. However, before he's allowed to enter Earth's atmosphere, he's stopped by the Demiurge. He describes this being as the "god" and creator of this world (separate from the higher source he experienced in heaven), and it is essentially in charge of what happens here. The Demiurge claims that any souls entering Earth must first agree to a contract, and then it goes on to describe certain aspects of the reality here in this world.

This Demiurge isn't exactly Satan, as it's not 100% dark or evil, but it does sound like the "fallen angel" that many of us are familiar with from religious texts. This being created this world as a sort of experiment where darkness is allowed to exist, presumably in order to experience things that can't be experienced in the perfect higher realms. However, for this experiment to work, a fine equilibrium needs to be maintained with a ratio of 2/3 light to 1/3 darkness. If the equilibrium is upset, then more light or dark beings/influences are added to the world. I got the impression that what we consider the Illuminati/TPTB/the cabal/etc. are consciously aware of this fact, and their task is to ensure the 1/3 level of darkness.

But then the Demiurge tells this person that the experiment is doomed to fail, and he showed the man what will happen. The equilibrium will at some point become irrevocably upset, and the Earth will plunge into a short period of extreme darkness. Groups of the elite will retreat to their underground bases in order to escape the destruction and the wrath of their evil deeds, but their plans will ultimately fail.

It is during this time of darkness and tribulation that the light is allowed to come flooding back into this world. Under the Demiurge, the Earth was in a sort of spell to help ensure the equilibrium could be maintained, but this essentially disappears when shit hits the fan and large masses begin to awaken. Those who survive the darkness and embrace the light will go on to create a world akin to heaven on earth. There will be no more money, suffering, disease, shitty jobs, etc. Instead it will be a world where we're allowed to create and pursue our passions before ultimately returning to Source.

There are many more fine details in the story that are worth reading, so check it out if you have the time. But hopefully I've covered the major points for all of you here. I found this story deeply moving as it matches up with many of my personal beliefs, and I'd love to know what you all think about it.
