  4. Analysis has shows that pollen and floral imprints found in the Shroud only exist in the area around Jerusalem.
  5. Chemical analysis of travertine aragonite calcium, found on in the Shroud's fabric, strongly suggests it was once in direct physical contact with one of Jerusalem's limestone caves or tombs.
