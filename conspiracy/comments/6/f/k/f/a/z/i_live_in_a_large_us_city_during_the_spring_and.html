I live in a large US City during the Spring and Summer I always notice an influx of Panhandle Gangs that station themselves on expressway exit ramps, and the exits of strip malls, and shopping centers. Recently I have begun to believe this group posing as a panhandle gang is actually a cult.

68  2017-06-06 by useyourimagination1

My first assumption was that these people are likely drug addicts and or are the same "homeless" people I notice downtown asking for change in the bar district area but after seeing them in action and using my people watching skills I began to notice some key differences between "downtown bums" and "roadway panhandlers". I also noticed that all of the roadway panhandlers seem to display many of the same characteristics and behavior.

They do not speak, do not smile, and avoid direct eye contact. They communicate with their patrons exclusively through hand gesturing i.e thumbs up, waving always keeping their hands below their shoulders, and also head nods, and I've noticed winks.

They are all "white", with only a very few women. All also appear to be ethnically nondescript for example no obvious Italians or other darker skinned and haired Anglo types. Everyone is of an average height and weight as well.

The cloths they wear seem to be purposefully nondescript as well absolutely no branding present on shirts and jackets not even small logos on the chest. I have also noticed that I have never seen any wearing Levi's Jeans which I love personably and always look to see if I can determine what type of jeans people are wearing. The standard dress seems to be light jeans, brown 4 eye boots rarely sneakers or combat or work style boots then heavy colored t-shirts or long sleeved shirts, with a denim or flannel jacket. Ball Hats only if it's raining and winter hats if it's cold. Never hoods over their heads and they do not wear sunglasses. The do not wear rings, watches, or brackets.

Most seem to be middle aged with only a few men I have noticed who look to be in their early thirties. It's seems that 40-55 or so is the overwhelming majority of ages.

For the most part they have a clean cut appearance most men are clean shaven. The haircut is nothing of note but seems to be fairly well kept no hair hanging over ears and etc. The few women do not have hair hanging over their faces and also wear no makeup whatsoever. They do not have dreads.

These people are clearly not Hippie Types or Gutter Punks. Even though they do not make eye contact, they do have clear eyes. They are not stoned or drunk. They do not stagger and they do not slouch, for people who are standing on their feet all day long they have surprisingly upright posture. They also appear to be well rested? they don't have dead tired eyes. Even though they are very reserved with their behavior it is clear; they have energy and are alert. They have a deep tan, with weathered faces and clear completions that suggests to me they spend time in a sunny and dry climate I feel like the Southwest would be the best bet as to where they are likely from or reside.

The "Downtown Bums" have a completely different approach they will engaged you in conversation many times at first pretending to strike up a conversation and then mention they lost their wallet or something to that effect. There seem to be two different classes of the "Downtown Bums" one is of a completely disheveled broken down dirty person and then one who is dressed slightly less disheveled and somewhat clean looking but wearing clothes slightly too big and many layers no matter the season.

I believe these people may be members of a cult and are possibly fulfilling some type of an initiation by taking part in the panhandling. This could be something of a training exercise to make certain the members have the drive, self control, and discipline to make it to the next step or role. They act deliberately and maintain what appears to be a code of conduct.
