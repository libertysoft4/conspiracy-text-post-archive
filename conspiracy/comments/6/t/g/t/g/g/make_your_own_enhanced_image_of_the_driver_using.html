Make your own enhanced image of the driver using GIMP

0  2017-08-13 by lessac

Here is how you can check things for yourself:

1 - Download the original image from here: http://www.mercurynews.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/confederate-monuments-prote1.jpg

2 - Get GIMP: https://www.gimp.org/ and open the image.

3 - Use the Colors->Retinex filter on the image.

4 - Done.

You will get something like this (the image on the center): https://i.imgur.com/toEDkQD.jpg

This is the original enhancement circulating on this sub with color correction: https://i.imgur.com/hV85QZL.jpg

More info on the Retinex filter: https://docs.gimp.org/en/plug-in-retinex.html
