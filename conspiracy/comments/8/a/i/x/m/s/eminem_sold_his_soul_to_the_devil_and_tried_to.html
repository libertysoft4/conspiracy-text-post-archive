My main reason for believing this is the song My Darling, released in 2009 on his comeback album after his hiatus. The song depicts a demon coming back to haunt Eminem and basically telling him that break time is over. Em tells the demon "I had you beat!" (he broke out of the deal with the devil) to which the demon responds "No, I was playing possom/Remember? I let you watch your little girls blossom/I gave you enough time, your soul's mine/I'm taking it back..." Throughout the song the demon tries to persuade Em to bleach his hair again and get back on stage. After Em rebels against this, the demon threatens to call Dr. Dre. Em shrugs it off, saying he already talked to Dre about this, but to Em's surprise Dre shows up and sides with the demon, showing Dre's complicity in the occult aspect of the industry. At this point, Em tries to shoot himself and end it for good (callback to Say Goodbye Hollywood) but the gunshot to the head does nothing, as the demon explains "You can't kill a spirit even if you tried to/You sold your soul to me, need I remind you?/You remember that night you prayed to God you'd give anything to get a record deal? Well, Dre signed you/This is what you wanted your whole life, Marshall". After these lines, we hear Em begin to sing along synonymously with the demon, showing that he has accepted his fate as an industry puppet.

It was after this album that Em became the industry puppet that he is today.
