iPhone listening to audio output?

8  2018-05-28 by lysii

Personally, I’ve always been a bit skeptical about the whole phone listening through mic thing, I’ve always chalked it up to coincidence, cognitive dissonance, and smart location/demographic based algorithms, but something happened last night that was truly bizarre and freaked me out.

I had come home from the gym and was relaxing petting my dog, when I felt like I had set my A/C too low as I was pretty cold. I wanted to lookup an ideal temperature, but wanted to see the conversion of my 23C to whatever it was in Fahrenheit.

I went to the homepage of my iPhone X and swiped right to the quick search bar and typed 23C. As I was only using one hand, I misclicked on the first Siri Suggested Webpage for 23C.

It opened the google news results about 23C weather in Ireland, from local Irish news sites. I live in Canada.

Literally 10 seconds before I searched this, and during, I was listening to Theo Von’s podcast and he had been listening to a call in voicemail from an Irish woman with an accent, talking about the difference between Irish and Scottish people. How fucking creepy is that?

When I search up 23c today, it just gives me generic American websites that talk about converting C to F.
