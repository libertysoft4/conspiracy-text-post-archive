I thought this theory was completely bullshit. Then I looked more into it... and I still think its probably bullshit. Although there quite a few weird coincidences I can't explain...

Yes, this part of the song is clearly about sending nudes... but it also obviously has a double entendre. Drake and most good rappers are known for these. Look how this verse ends: "Why you keep on shootin' if you know that nigga dead? (Skrr)"

Also this part of the song repeats "(Skrr)"... the only other part of the song that uses "(Skrr)" is when he says "Made me wanna buy a vest and a TEC (Skrr)" A TEC is a TEC-9... a semi-automatic pistol. Immediately following the gun makes it clear that "(Skrr)" represents a car pulling up/shooting. Look up "(Skrr)" it is literally gang slang for a car pulling up/shooting. Strange that it is only at these two parts of the song... and that a car pulled up and shot X. Also strange that in his music video Drake has a couple gunshot sounds for when the speakers blow...

"SMS" is short for send my shooters.

One meaning of "triple X" is obviously "XXXTentacion", play the song on Spotify and even there you will see that in the lyric description it says this is most likely an XXXTentacion diss. You can also hear "x...x...x" whispered before this part of the song.

The part in the song leading up to this has nothing to do with sending nudes to his ex. Instead it is about someone trying to "check him", which is a double entendre for challenging him as the king and also for accusing him of something (X said Drake stole his song, X said Drake's mom has an STD, X may have told Pusha T about Drake's baby). "You tryna check?

The next part is a threat about how this is real life and there are real repercussions (getting killed?) for trying to check Drake. "This is real life, niggas think we playin' chess"

Drake is acting like something evil took over him and made him do something cold. So what's next? Jump up out the bed like I’m possessed" ... Got a lot of blood and it's cold"

The whole song is about sending shooters and ordering a hit. "I'm upset Hunnid thousand on my head, it's disrespect" ... "They keep tryna get me for my soul (for my soul)" [This could also hint "they" tempted him to lose his soul by ordering a hit]

XXXTentacion was shot in the heart, that is why he did not bleed out significantly and instantly slumped after being shot. There was no visible shots or blood in the video of his deceased body slumped in the car. Witnesses saw him get shot below the neck/on the chest. "That's the only time I ever shoot below the neck (skrr)"

He was shot multiple times in the chest, even after being hit in the heart. Witnesses heard multiple gunshots. Lack of bleeding signifies that he was already dead before the rest of the shots, or at the very least the shooters knew he was already dead. When you get shot multiple times in your center of mass it is typically because someone wants you dead, not just to rob you. "Why you keep on shootin' if you know that nigga dead? (Skrr)"

Drake had major beef with XXXTentacion. Drake is close with real criminals/killers. Drake wants to appear tough. Drake's label owner Birdman has even been accused of ordering a hit in 2015 by one of the attempted murderers after he was caught. During one of his concerts Kanye said that Jay Z and probably other big names have shooters (he mentioned Drake right before Jay Z, Drake's next album has Jay Z on it). One of X's last live videos is him anticipating his death and basically saying goodbye. X also posted online that if anyone kills him it is Drake. Weird coincidence? I think its just a coincidence.. but sure there is a small possibility.
