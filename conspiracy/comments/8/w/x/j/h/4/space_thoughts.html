Space thoughts

1  2018-07-08 by G10RG1O

Yesterday, i looked outside of my window and saw the moon. For my whole entire life i’ve seen it as “the moon”, a ball of light in the sky. It didn’t take longer before i started thinking about how beautiful it is.

We live on a planet, pretty much isolated from everything that happens outside of earth. Here we have so much, things that just seem normal to us like houses to live in, cars, jobs, or just a society in general, and yet we are so small. Isn’t that just amazing?

Sometimes, i don’t realise we just live on a ball with darkness and the unknown surrounding us, everything is nothing and nothing is everything. And yet we still exist

Look out of your window or listen to the sounds you hear and think about, how has everything come to be?

Are there places similiar to ours?

Imagine a planet like earth but 3 times as large, how does it look to you? Are there continents? What do the landscapes look like?

And if they would look out of their window or listen to the sounds they can hear, would they ask the same?

Another thing i find impressive is how time slows down in space. You can go to space and come back younger that your twin. Even though time, isn’t even a thing by itself.

Do we matter, in the universe?

Are we something worth of living?

And who decides if we are or not?

What, are we?

(Giorgio, 15 y/o)
