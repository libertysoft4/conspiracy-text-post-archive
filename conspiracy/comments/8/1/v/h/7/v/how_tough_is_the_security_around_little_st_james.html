I've spent some time on St. Thomas. You can easily boat to Little Saint James, though I'm not sure about what kind of security you might run into if you tried to land. It's too far to swim, plus there are major shipping lanes that cross between St. Thomas and the St. James islands which present a hazard.

All beaches on St. Thomas are open to the public. Closest launch areas would be Secret Harbor which has a small dock if you have your own boat, or Sapphire Beach which has a more extensive dock. The Ritz sits nearby too, but I don't recall them having launch facilities, plus they're under major reconstruction right now - the hurricanes took their roof off. I'm almost certain there's boat rentals at Sapphire where you can commission someone to take you out to look around. If not there, then you can definitely find that service in Red Hook.
