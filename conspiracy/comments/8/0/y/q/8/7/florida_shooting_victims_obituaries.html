Florida shooting victims obituaries.

16  2018-02-28 by Tunderbar1

You all might find this a tad gruesome, but I found something that I think is odd, peculiar, strange. I looked up the Florida shooting victims names, then I looked up their obituaries.

I'd say at least half of the obits, probably closer to two thirds of them looked like they were cobbled together by people not related to the victims. With little or no input from family members. They read like a quick third party summation with quotes about the person actually taken from newspaper reports. Nothing loving and personal at all.

Several of them actually read like what you would expect the family to write. Actual personal information about the persons life, their character, interests, etc.

So.... what to conclude? I don't know. Read this stuff and comment.
