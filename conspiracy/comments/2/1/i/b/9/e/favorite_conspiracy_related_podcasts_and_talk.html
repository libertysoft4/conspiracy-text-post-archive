Coast to coast is fluff. If you're into that new age/alien stuff- Jordan Maxwell, Jim Mars, etc- David Icke the reptillian dude is your guy. That's a pretty easy leap over to Alex Jones. There's a lot of fear mongering and talk about the "illuminati" along those avenues, though. If you want something on that illuminatti theme with some good history to back it up, check out Alan Watt's old podcasts (not to be confused with Alan Watts- who is also very enlightening)

If you're looking for analysis- Gerald Celente, Max Keiser and No Agenda, Ben Swann, Boiling Frogs are decent.

Frankly, when you listen to any of this stuff, you have to be discerning and take things with a grain of salt. A lot of them simplify and are fairly cynical. I find it safest to just vary my news sources and do my own analysis and deconstruction.
