Climate change is too vague a term. It should be broken down into:

  • Natural climate change - ie the earth has gone through hot and cold periods for thousands of years.

  • Man made climate change - human impact on the earth climate and co2 emissions

  • Environmental damage - eg destruction of forests, food chains

  • Pollution - eg plastic in our oceans, no clean air

Each is different, each has an impact on our planet, and not all of them are linked. It confuses matters when they are all lumped together, IMHO.
