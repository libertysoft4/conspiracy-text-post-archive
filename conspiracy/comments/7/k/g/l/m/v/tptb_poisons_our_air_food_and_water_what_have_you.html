Oh I forgot to mention under 20g of net carbs, which just means you subtract the fiber from the overall number of carbs because fiber isn't really digestible. So that may make things a bit easier.

So about the sweet potato, one cup of sweet potato has 27g of carbs with 4g of fiber, so net carbs for one cup is 23g. So while you could get away with like a half cup of sweet potato in a day, you'd have to be very careful for the rest of the day. I'd try to substitute the sweet potato with avocado.
