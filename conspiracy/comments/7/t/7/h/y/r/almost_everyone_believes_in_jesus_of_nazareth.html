0  2018-01-26 by r3dr4gon

Even most atheists and agnostics believe that historically there existed a man from Nazareth who claimed to be more than just an ordinary carpenter, whose choice of words and actions eventually led to his death by crucifixion at the hands of the Romans and Jewish authorities.

But if you have faith that he is the Messiah, the Savior, the Son of God, then you can also have faith that his death was the doorway for God to deliver salvation to the World, and that we can share in the same resurrection as Christ if we put our faith in him and our trust in God. This is a great hope to have in this life, the hope of an eternal afterlife with a loving God and Savior.

His life is the reason why we should believe, he lived a life full of love and grace, showed us what is possible for humans if they are united with God, full of his perfect love. His sacrifice on the cross is also why we should believe, because he willingly gave up his own life to save ours. He had many followers in those days who believed he was their Messiah, who were willing to fight to protect him, but he allowed himself to be arrested and crucified, because this was the greatest act of love mankind would ever witness, the sacrifice of the holy Son of God for the sins of mankind, the pathway opening up for humans to be born again as children of God.
