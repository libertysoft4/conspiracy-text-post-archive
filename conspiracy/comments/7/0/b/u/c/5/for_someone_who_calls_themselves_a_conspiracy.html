61  2017-09-15 by samuel_petard

The Truth is the only thing that matters.

I got interested in conspiracy theories, because I realized that the normal mode of information I am getting are biased and are not a completely reliable source of information.

When the concept of conspiracy theories started, it was motivated with getting the truth. Nowadays as it has gone more main stream, it has also been tainted with politics.

Politics has its own place, but it cannot supersede the truth.

The stated aim of a conspiracy theory culture shall be to know the truth, challenge it , refine it and again challenge it.

It is a scientific process, not a political one.

I lean right, but if I am following a conspiracy sub, I would be interested in knowing all the truth about any person on the right, no matter how much I support them. If the truth challenges my ideological beliefs and my political position, that still remains the truth

Same goes for the left or any other political position.

It is ridiculous to challenge and redefine the truth due to political leanings.

If someone says something truthful but undesirable about someone or something we believe in, we shall look into the veracity of the claim, not how that claim makes us feel.

The Truth doesn't care about feelings, it is neutral and that is what we shall seek, irrespective of the various social constructs that divide us.
