Conspiracy theorist how do you manage your mental health?

29  2018-02-07 by colt4594

This is not a conspiracy thread and I am not trying to attack any of you guys by any means.

I found myself when the vegas shooting went down I wanted answers. My friends, friends, boyfriend was killed in the shooting and it just seemed to surreal. Even so I never met this guy I just found it crazy and incredibly sad what happened and wanted to know the true motive. As we know still hasn't been released exactly. I was in this sub a lot and at the same time getting consumed by other conspriacys. Watching lots of stuff on YouTube.

While this was happening I started feeling really angry and getting depressed about the world in general. I found it hard to enjoy life. After a month or two I stopped being in here as much and stopped with the youtube videos. It was starting to make me miserable along with other things going on in my life

I've always took everything with a grain of salt. Tried to read between the lines as much as I can. Especially as I've gotten older. I realize so much of what we consume is trying to promote certain agendas and or propaganda. That being said the conspiracy mentality was not healthy for my mental health.

I still come in this sub here and there. I still watch some youtube videos (mainly Shawn dawson's conspiracy videos) but I felt like if I didn't stop diving in deep into everything I would still be in that miserable state.

How do you guys manage? Have any of you feel/felt the same way? If so what do you do to keep a positive mindset?
