The Vegas Shooting, lets stay focused-

52  2017-10-09 by mjr133113

I love the dedication and effort that this sub has put in to trying to uncover the truth. This is a legitimately growing on people outside of this community, and the average people are highly suspicious of it. Given that, I think it’s important that we focus on asking the important questions (because it’s still so early on), rather than passing off rash conclusions.

Here are some of the most significant questions I have, and that I think we should be asking:

-Why is the story regarding the note changing? First it was suicide, then trajectories, then phone numbers? Why is the story so inconsistent?

-Why were there so many eyewitness accounts of multiple shooters, such as people claiming shooters at other casinos, or witnesses at Mandalay saying moments after that there were multiple gunmen?

-Why did Paddock have so many weapons? Understandable to bring extras in case of overheating or jams, but 19 guns for one person is excessive.

-What was the motive for a retired, wealthy man, with no history of mental illness, to try mass murder hundreds of people?

-What evidence exists to suggest he had an escape plan, and if so, why didn’t he activate it?

Please add any legitimate questions, I know I’m missing a bunch.

And finally, when talking to people who aren’t from this Conspiracy community, let’s make sure we do more asking then theorizing and drawing conclusions (at this state). I’m all for getting the theories out here on this sub, but asking valid and unanswered questions is a better strategy for persuading and spreading curiosity to the average person.
