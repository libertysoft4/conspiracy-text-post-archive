Veteran's day is Saturday, November 11th. Veteran's day started out as Armistice Day, the end of World War I. In the US this day morphed into Veterans Day, a day to celebrate all soldiers, living and dead. Knowing the history behind Veteran's day helps me appreciate it for what it is.
