You can get more with honey then you can with vinegar

1  2018-09-20 by Jango139

I woke up on the 10th anniversary of 9/11, I was 26-years-old and had already served in the United States Marine Corps. I was a true believer and would have defended my existing worldview without reverse, but I hadn’t been woken up yet and was operating strictly on my 12 years of public school programming that was significantly reinforced by the Marines.

I often see many who have woken up being judgmental of those who still cannot see. My brother in law is awake but is a prick in his delivery to people who are unfamiliar or are resistant and likes to call them names, like “sheeple.”

We have all been there because we’re all victims of the same systems of command and control starting when we’re babies. Kind of hard to judge an unwitting victim or one suffering from denial, again, because we all have been there and felt those powerful emotions as we woke up and accepted the new reality of things.

Just try to be mindful of the Golden Rule, please, it really can take us far — particularly in the type of emotionally heated and heavy stuff we get into here.
