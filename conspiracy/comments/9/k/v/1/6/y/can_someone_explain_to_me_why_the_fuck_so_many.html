If Facebook is a carrier, then they have to be neutral about what appears on their platform - and they're not responsible for it. If they're a publisher, then they are responsible for everything said thereon. Right now they're somewhere in between. They exercise a publisher's discretion in censoring politically incorrect voices, but they receive a carrier's leeway for illegal misuse of the platform, e.g. if you libel someone on Facebook, Facebook is not held responsible provided they delete the libel in a timely fashion. If they were a publisher, they would be responsible for everything they publish, and so would have to have everything approved by lawyers.
